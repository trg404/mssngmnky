Stiegl Radler: It's "Beer"

Long days standing out in the hot sun are nothing new in the world. Mowing the grass or even standing in line at Disney World make one very thirsty and the smarter of us decide not to drink alcohol at that point. Thank God there is Stiegl Raddler. With an alcohol percentage at a raging 2.5 percent it’s not like you’re actually drinking. The taste of candied grapefruit and a mild hint of beer at the end it’s perfect if you’re not into drinking or shouldn’t be.

After waiting in line for over an hour to ride around in circles in a large plastic elephant you’re shreds of dignity are firmly long behind you. Walking up to a vendor, show five types of Id, and a bright blue wrist band which the cashier now scans deducting way to much money out of an account that will come back to bite you later, you receive your purchase, a plastic cup with a circus peanut orange fizzy soda with just a hint of alcohol in it. You sip it with the greatest of care because of the price. Walking down to yet another animatronic monstrosity and loving every minute of the “beer” you’ve purchased. Screw being an adult today I want to be the biggest slightly buzzed kid in the entire park.
