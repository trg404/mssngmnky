Dale's Pale Ale

A beer with a lying problem Dale’s Pale Ale is a hop centric tour of what the western United States has to offer. The new brewery in Brevard North Carolina doesn’t really let you know that this is a Colorado born and bred recipe for shoving enough hops down the beers throat to make a beer drinker take second notice of this easy sipping IPA and rough as hell pale ale. Selling it to customers without a warning of this hop laden can of goodness is akin to not letting someone know that you’re cop while looking for a weed hook-up. It’s a damn good IPA and not to be missed but be warned you’re in for a shock if you’re expecting a pale ale.

PALE ALE

  • IBU: 30-45
  • ABV 4.5-6.2%
  • SRM 5-14
  • OG: 1.045 - 1.060
