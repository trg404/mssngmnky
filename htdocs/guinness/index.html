Irish as St. Patrick's Day

Out for spring break because the your city has exploded in an Irish car bomb of green and drunkard, you walk down the crowded streets remember a time when you were in Ireland and can’t remember seeing anything close to this rendition of Apocalypse now. You walk into a bar with a motivation of “can’t beat them join them” instead of ride of the valkyries you’re assaulted to Massachusetts accented claims of how irish they are and bands claiming similar concepts. In a sea of horrible stereotypes and probably well beyond borderlined racist accusations the bartender asks for your order “Guinness Please.”
