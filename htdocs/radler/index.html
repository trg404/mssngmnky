Radler

Radler’s are a great alternative to drinking. It’s true at 2.5% alcohol it’s not like you’re gonna get drunk off them but they’re a relaxing take the edge off drink that goes great with the summer months where you’re more concentrated on making sure the lawn isn’t withering to a crispy brown by the time you have to get the damn mower out again. A mixture of lemonade, soda or some other citrus related beverage and a beer, radlers are a great inventions from Germany.
